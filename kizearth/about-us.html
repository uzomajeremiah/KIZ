About us

Kizearth Technologies

Kizearth with RC:1412220 is an IT solutions company based in Nigeria. We are an organisation of highly skilled ICT professionals providing expert solutions to various ICT problems by means of software design, development, implementation and management.

We provide cutting edge technologies and solutions to Nigerian businesses and individuals. We are proud and excited to work with a wide and diverse range of clients in different sectors. Since our inception, we have evolved from a small organization to a company providing enterprise ICT services to mid range and large scale companies across Nigeria. Part of our strength is in our ability to deliver high quality work on schedule.

Kizearth is a full custom solutions firm. As such, it’s our business to quickly adapt to meet our clients’ diverse needs… and we have a proven track record of doing just that. we are experts in delivering Technology Solutions. Our solutions are cost effective, easy to use and adaptive for next level growth. These solutions provide the platform for an agile ICT environment that drives organizations towards set objectives.

At Kizearth, our primary concern is client satisfaction, and in order to achieve this, we bring together ICT experts who have gained vast knowledge and experience in different areas of the ICT to handle every project.
